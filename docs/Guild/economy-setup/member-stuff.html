Guild Member Commands

Member Stuff

Member engagement is a vital aspect of managing a successful guild. Here's a few things to do in the guild

Basic Commands

  • /guild pay: Distribute payments to members of your guild.
    • Usage: Specify the member and amount to pay. Example: /guild pay @Member 100.
  • /guild inventory: View your guild’s current stock of items.
    • Usage: specify a member to see their inventory otherwise leave blank. Example: /guild inventory [user]
  • /guild shop buy: Allow members to purchase items from the guild shop.
    • Usage: Specify the item name and quantity. Example: /guild shop buy iron 2.

Contract Filling

Contracts are a structured way to gather resources or assign tasks to members. They incentivize participation and help achieve guild goals efficiently.

  • Purpose: Engage members by assigning specific tasks or resource-gathering goals.
  • Commands:
    • /contract create: Define a new contract specifying the required item, quantity, and reward (global or local currency).
  • Tips:
    • Offer competitive rewards to attract member participation.
    • Method to gather materials and other needed items.

Gambling & Casino Features

Guild members can gamble and test their luck with these commands.

  • /blackjack: Play a game of blackjack using your guild’s local currency.
    • Usage: Specify the amount you want to bet with. Ex: /blackjack 100.
  • /roulette: Try your luck at roulette with different betting options.
    • Usage: Specify the amount you want to bet with. Ex: /roulette 100.
    • Upcoming Features:
    • /slots: A slot machine game will soon be added.
    • /poker: Poker games are in development and coming soon.
