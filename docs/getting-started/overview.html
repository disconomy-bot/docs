Disconomy Bot Documentation - Overview

Introduction

Disconomy is a Discord bot designed to provide guilds with control over their own local economies while seamlessly integrating into a global economic ecosystem. With Disconomy, your server can create, manage, and grow its economy in a way that reflects your unique community needs.

Key Features

  • Hybrid Economy System: Combine local guild-based economy control with global economy integration.
  • Customizable Currency: Set up unique currencies that reflect your guild's identity.
  • Role-Based Permissions: Assign specific roles to manage economic features with ease.
  • Global Exchange: Trade and convert local currencies into global currency and vice versa.
  • Comprehensive Audit Logs: Maintain transparency with detailed event logs.

Guild and Global Economy

Disconomy offers a hybrid approach to economic management:

  • Guild-Based Economy: Build your server's local economy with features like custom shops, member wages, and chat-based currency earning systems.
  • Global Economy: Extend your economy beyond your guild by connecting to a larger network of servers. Participate in resource trading, global markets, and collective economic activities.

This dual-system allows for an immersive and flexible economic experience, allowing guilds to grow locally while contributing to a larger global ecosystem.
